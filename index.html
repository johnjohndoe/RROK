Welcome to RROK (Random Rides of Kindness)

Ticketteilen.org

RROK (Random Rides of Kindness) is a part of an initiative to promote ticketteilen.org, a ticket sharing system for public transportation in Berlin which helps provide a method of transport to thoes who aren't able to afford transportation. Owners of BVG and S-Bahn monthly passes can let someone share the train with them on certain times (evening, weekends). This website allows thoes who have shared a ride with someone, to record their journey

Shared a ride - Share your experience

RROK is Growing

RROK begin as an idea from Geeklist #Hack4good 0.5, inspired by the ticketteilen-campaign by Naturfreunde Berlin. It allows monthly ticket holders who have "RROKed" (allowed someone share a ride with them) to record their journey. The record the start station and the end station into the website. This information is recoreded and you receive feedback about your good deed. You can also find out how your neighbourhood and transport line scores against others by using the heatmap.
